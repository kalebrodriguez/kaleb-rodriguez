# Content Sources

The copy on this website was written specifically for the site and then
reviewed for accuracy. It was informed only by **public-facing** material about
Kaleb Rodriguez. No private documents, file contents, or local file paths are
reproduced here or in the repository.

## General source categories

- **Public résumé** — education, experience, activities, skills, honors, and
  certifications.
- **Public LinkedIn profile** — current roles, titles, dates, education, and
  listed publications (used to keep the site accurate and up to date).
- **Public project summaries** — high-level descriptions of personal projects
  (DigitalTwin, Kora, ClearCredit, MedAlert, Posture+, Cramb).
- **Public GitHub repositories** — project names and one-line descriptions from
  publicly visible repositories under `github.com/kalebrodriguez`.
- **Research descriptions** — plain-language summaries of research interests and
  ongoing work, kept deliberately general.

## Intentionally excluded

To protect privacy and research confidentiality, the following were **not**
used or published, even where source material contained them:

- Home address and personal phone number
- Personal email correspondence
- Birth date and any government or school ID numbers
- Passwords, tokens, API keys, or configuration files
- Private application essays and personal statements
- Academic records not intended for publication (e.g., exact GPA)
- Unpublished or confidential research data and figures
- Health, clinical, or patient-related details
- Any private information about other individuals

Ongoing research is described only in broad terms. Specific unpublished
results, datasets, and internal documents are omitted by design.
